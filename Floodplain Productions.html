Floodplain Productions

A seemingly fictitious, virtual record label, providing the fertile seeds and solvent for a plethora of modern producers and musicians, including everybody's favorite cartilaginous predator, The FloodShark. The Floodplains: generating sonic waves for the good of the realm.
