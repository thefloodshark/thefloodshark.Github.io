Floodplain Productions

A seemingly fictitious, virtual record label, providing the fertile seeds and solvent for a plethora of modern producers and musicians, including everybody's favorite cartilaginous predator, The FloodShark. Floodplain Productions - generating sonic waves for ardent audio-surfers.

Production resources and links

A collection of the best music production, playing, and theory resources I've come across:
  • soundonsound.com - features a variety of detailed, high quality audio, music, and production-related articles, as well as user forums
  • gearslutz.com/ - a phenomenal site for audio production & engineering questions and discussions
  • https://archive.org/details/GuideToMixing - an incredibly useful and insightful guide to mixing and mastering, from the perspective of an electronic/synthetic artist. Nick Thomas
