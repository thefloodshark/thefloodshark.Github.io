The Floodplains

An eclectic media site – home to the virtual, seemingly fictitious record label Floodplain Productions and the ever-fluid sonic waves of The FloodShark. A home to memes, madness, and music, including original content in the form of poetry, varied, miscellaneous articles, and comparative, running lists. Naturally, The Floodplains contains always-free downloads of The FloodShark's entire discography.

About the Author

  • Holds a Bachelor's and Master's degree in Environmental Engineering from the University of Florida, specializing in water systems

  • An audiophile and producer/composer currently surfing under the soon-to-be infamous pseudonym The FloodShark - the bloodthirsty author of the majority of this site's content
