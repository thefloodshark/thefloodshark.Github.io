    The FloodShark - a Saudi Arabian-raised, Florida-based producer/composer turned digital, cartilaginous thresher surfing the sonic waves with an eclectic musical style.

    “Aquabots / Robots Acuáticos” + “Stardrops / Gotas de Estrellas” posits a varied, two-piece robotic-aquatic single rooted in contemporary electronic and ambient styles, serving as a precursor to the upcoming LP, The FloodShark / El Tiburón de la Inundación. Complete with visualizer video 🤖💧
