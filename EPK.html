Biography

The FloodShark: A Florida-based producer/composer turned cartilaginous, blood-thirsty predator surfing the sonic waves with an eclectic musical style. After spending the first 8 years of its life in Saudi Arabia, followed by the next 10 in coastal Virginia, the Shark's taken residency along the continental shelf of the Sunshine State.

The FloodShark's music lies on the more experimental side of electronic, positing a modern fusion of instrumental hip-hop, ambient, plunderphonics, field recordings, and Western classical music. Musical themes include political and social resistance, the realities of environmental degradation, and the omnipotence of nature, among others. Prominent collaborators include legendary Super Smash Bros. player Hungrybox and professional voice actor Nazeeh Tarsha (Code Geass, One Piece).
