Recommended Applications and Extensions

  • Desktop
    • ClipJump - increases the number of allowed entries and size of the clipboard
    • CCleaner - remove applications, digital trash, and more
    • Clover - adds tab functionality to Windows explorer similar to Google Chrome
    • VLC Media Player - a flexible, lightweight media player with tons of other perks
    • Search Everything - easily search your computer's files
  • Mobile
    • Tasker
    • YouTube Vanced - a modded YouTube app for Android with lock screen playback. Since playback is capable even when the phone is locked, it can double as a music player
  • Browser Extensions (Chrome)
    • U-Block Origin - still one of the best adblockers around
    • Tampermonkey - insert JavaScript into web pages. Amazing automation potential
    • OneTab - consolidate and save tab links at the click of a button
    • Pushbullet - easy way to send information across devices in browser
    • Linkclump - customized clicks and mouse movements to open multiple tabs at once
    • I'm not a robot captcha clicker - automated captcha clicker (the irony)
    • Full Page Screen Capture - screecap the entirety of a web page
    • Katalon Automation Recorder - way to record actions and replay them. A useful browser automation tool
    • Autofill - one of the best Autofill extensions out there
    • Imagus - creates a pop out window of an image upon hover and avoids the pitfalls of HoverZoom
    • Download All Images - download all the images in a tab with customization and selection options
    • Save Tabbed Images - downloads images across tabs at the click of a button. May require conversion for extension to work in browser
  • Browser Extensions (Firefox)
    • Check4Change - notifications (including email) when elements or text on a webpage change
    • Snaplinks - Mozilla equivalent of LinkClump
