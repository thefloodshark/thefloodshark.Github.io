Classical Corner

Resources

  • Classical Net - contains a variety of classical reviews, articles, recommendations, and biographies. Their "Basic Repertoire" lists are excellent listening guides
  • Presto Classical - a site dedicated to cataloging and selling thousands of classical recordings. Great for searching award-winning interpretations
  • Deutsche Grammophon - an important classical music label. The website includes recordings, news, biographies, concerts, and much more
  • Gramophone - fantastic source for classical reviews
  • MusOpen - free, quality music books, sheet music, and more
  • ClassicFM - a useful classical resource and digital radio
  • Digital Dream Door - a list of the greatest classical composers and their magnum opi. The site includes a variety of lists spanning various genres
  • /r/classicalresources - a wonderful subreddit and collection of user-submitted classical resources
  • Music Theory Online - a music theory journal in publication since 1993
  • Brahms Listening Guides - guides to dozen of Brahms's works
  • Orchestral Instruments - sheet music and playing examples
  • /r/classicalmusic - a subreddit for all things classical
  • Classical CD Guide - a website dedicated to cataloging classical recordings
  • Naxos Recommendations - 120 recommended classical recordings by the Naxos classical label
  • Scarlatti's 559 Sonatas Ranked - a detailed ranking of Scarlatti's keyboard sonatas
  • Talk Classical - forums for everything classical
