Floodplain Productions

An enigmatic, virtual record label, providing the fertile seeds and solvent for a plethora of modern producers and musicians, including everybody's favorite cartilaginous predator, The FloodShark. Floodplain Productions - surfing the sonic waves with an eclectic musical style.

Production resources and links

A collection of excellent music production, playing, and theory resources:

  • soundonsound.com - features a variety of detailed, high quality audio, music, and production-related articles, as well as user forums
  • gearslutz.com/ - a phenomenal site for audio production & engineering questions and discussions
  • Nick Thomas's Guide to Mixing - an incredibly useful and insightful guide to mixing and mastering, from the electronic/synthetic perspective
  • SonicBloom - All things Ableton Live, including Push, Max, and free downloads
  • Bedroom Producers Blog
  • /r/AudioEngineering - a wonderful subreddit for all things sound
  • /r/WeAretheMusicMakers - another great subreddit for discussing, sharing, and collaborating
  • findsongtempo.com - a useful tool that provides musical details (tempo, key, time signature, etc.) for any searched song/piece
  • music-map.com - search the history of music and related artists and scenes with ease
  • harmony.org - an excellent music theory resource
